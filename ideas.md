## SELECTED: Option B — "Crisp Plush"

This is the design that most faithfully captures the Figma Plush aesthetic:
- Crisp white canvas with deep forest green authority
- Photography as atmosphere (Unsplash aerial city/architecture shots)
- Large bold numerals for data hierarchy
- Liberation Serif for editorial gravitas
- Work Sans for clean UI legibility
- Bento-style asymmetric dashboard grids
- Smooth, purposeful interactions — nothing decorative
